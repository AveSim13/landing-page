Games I Play

Life is Strange

These games fuck me up. For weeks after playing I can't stop thinking of them. I spend my life trying to find my own Rachel Amber to be with ♥

Kena: Bridge of Spirits

This is one of the most beautiful games I've ever played and I desperately want sequels. I love Kena to death. So much so I'm planning a Kena tattoo!

Master of Orion

For literal decades, I have played Master of Orion. I have spent ages trying to find a game as perfect as MoO2. I have yet to do so. I was extremely excited for the new Conquer the Stars game, but they made the same mistakes all newer 4X games make. So I still return to MoO2. It might be as old as I am, but it's a masterpiece.

Civilizations

Perhaps the closest modern game to MoO, I do enjoy me some Civilization games here and there. It's a cozy game for me. One I can return to time and again to sink some time. I'm a bit of a pacifist and a communist though, so I hope you're prepared to see that play style!

DK 64

You'll probably see me try to play Donkey Kong 64 here and there. It's another one of those games from my childhood that I've forever tried to find a modern verision of, but to no avail.

Call of Duty: Warzone

I'll be real with you. CoD isn't my fave. But it's the game a lot of my friends play, and I love playing games with my friends. Someday I hope to have a lil gaming community to play tons of games with. But for now, we'll stick with my IRL friends' games.

Creation is survival, and I'm just looking to survive. My streams are a place for everyone. As an LGBT, autistic woman, I want to build a community of kind people, where we can all come to have fun, discuss respectfully, and love completely.
